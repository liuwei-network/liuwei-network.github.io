About

I study networks where terrestrial assumptions no longer hold.

I am an Assistant Researcher at Zhongguancun Laboratory. I received my Ph.D. from Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Yuanjie Li, after completing my bachelor’s degree at Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications.

My research spans satellite and cellular networks, with a current focus on direct-to-cell satellite systems. I build and measure real systems to understand mobility, security, routing, and infrastructure at constellation scale.

Research

LEO networked systems

  • Routing
  • Space core networks
  • Large-scale measurement

Revisiting cellular architecture as the network expands across rapidly moving and heterogeneous infrastructure.
