Biography

Hey! I am Wei Liu, currently an Assistant Researcher at Zhongguancun Laboratory. I received my Ph.D. degree from Tsinghua University, where I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. Yuanjie Li. Previously, I obtained my bachelor's degree from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT).

My research interests lie in satellite networks and cellular networks. My current research focuses on direct-to-cell satellite networks.

I actively maintain an open bibliography page for satellite network papers, projects, and resources on Github. Check here for details.
