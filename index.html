Biography

Hey! I am Wei Liu, currently a PhD candidate in Institute for Network Sciences and Cyberspace at Tsinghua University (THU), and fortunate to be advised by Prof. Yuanjie Li. Previously, I obtained my bachelor's degree from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT).


My research interests fall in satellite network and its security. The current research focuses on direct-to-cell satellite network.


I actively maintain an open bibliography page for satellite network papers, projects, and resources on Github. Check here for details.


I am actively seeking job opportunities in both **academia and industry** starting in 2026. If my research and background align with your interests, I'd be delighted to connect. Feel free to email me at lw21 [at] mails.tsinghua.edu.cn
