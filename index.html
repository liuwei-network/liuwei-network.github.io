Wei Liu 刘威

Assistant Researcher
Zhongguancun Laboratory

I am a network systems researcher working on satellite and cellular networks. My current research focuses on direct-to-cell satellite networks, including their architecture, mobility, measurement, and security.

I received my Ph.D. from Tsinghua University, where I was advised by Prof. Yuanjie Li. Previously, I received my bachelor’s degree from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications.

Email Google Scholar GitHub

Research

My research interests include:

  • Direct-to-cell satellite networks
  • LEO satellite networked systems
  • Cellular network architecture and security

I also maintain Awesome Satellite Networking, an open collection of papers, projects, datasets, testbeds, and tools for satellite networking.
